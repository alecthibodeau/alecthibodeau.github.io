about

Hello! I’m a builder. I’ve built art and design pieces, from pencil drawings to a large sculpture in New York City’s public art collection. And I've built websites to share these pieces and hone my software-writing skills. My specialty is front-end web development with a strong emphasis on responsive web design.

languages, technologies & techniques

I work regularly with JavaScript, TypeScript, Angular, Angular testing, Angular Material, Material Design, Bootstrap, CSS3 / Sass, Python, MySQL, HTML5, RxWeb, Chrome DevTools, Slack, Git / GitHub, Atlassian (Bitbucket, Jira, Confluence), Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. I also have experience with jQuery, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Mongoose and Heroku.
